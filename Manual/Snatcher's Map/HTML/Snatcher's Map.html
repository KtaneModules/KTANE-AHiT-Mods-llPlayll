On the Subject of Snatcher's Map

Did you know you could unlock a Luigi by completing every Death Wish on the first attempt? I don't even know what that is, but it has to be genuine, I read it online.

Initially, this module will contain the first word of a phrase.

To solve the module, submit the correct Death Wish stamp on the map, depending on the phrase.

Tapping the module in between timer ticks will cycle through the phrase's words, with the first word being gray. Holding the module over a timer tick and releasing will enter submission mode. (you can't go back to the phrase without striking, so be carefull with your presses!)

All words in this module are written in the Nyakuza font:

ABCDEFGHIJKLM
ABCDEFGHIJKLM
NOPQRSTUVWXYZ
NOPQRSTUVWXYZ
1234567890'!:
1234567890'!:
,-().
,-().

The phrase you're cycling through is either the Main or Bonus Goals, or the Description of a Death Wish on the map. (Refer to Appendix D34THW15H for information about the different Death Wishes)

Finally, to solve the module, press any Death Wish on the map that has the module's phrase as a bit of information as stated above. (The Appendix offers a clip of the position of the stamp.)
